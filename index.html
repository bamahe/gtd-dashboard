üîÅ Weekly Review ‚Äî The Detailed Breakdown

Block 60‚Äì90 min every Friday afternoon or Sunday evening. This is the #1 habit that makes GTD work.

PHASE 1
Get Clear (20 min)

Empty all inboxes ‚Äî Notion, email, Obsidian, paper, phone notes, voicemails. Process every item through the decision tree. Get to zero.

PHASE 2
Get Current (25 min)

Review every active project ‚Äî does each one have a clear Next Action? Review Next Actions lists. Review Waiting For list ‚Äî follow up on anything overdue. Review calendar (past week + next 2 weeks). Review Someday/Maybe ‚Äî anything ready to activate?

PHASE 3
Get Creative (15 min)

What new projects or ideas came up this week? Any new Someday/Maybe items? Brain dump anything floating in your head. Think about what would make next week great.

PHASE 4
Plan Next Week (10 min)

Identify your Big 3 for next week. Time-block major work sessions. Flag any deadlines or hard landscape items. Set your Monday morning MITs.
